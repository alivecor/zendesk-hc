Does KardiaBand have a battery and can it be changed?

Yes, it is a 3v CR1620 coin cell battery. It is user replaceable.

To open the device you will need a Torx T3 or Philips 000 screwdriver to remove the screws in the battery door.

If the device is too small for you to open it yourself, it is recommended that the device be taken to a jeweler or watch repair person to change the battery.Â
