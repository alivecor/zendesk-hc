What's the difference between basic and premium for KardiaMobile users?

KardiaMobile does not require any subscription of any kind. EKGs may be printed or emailed an unlimited number of times right after you take the recording. Kardia Premium membership includes advanced services and features detailed in the table below. All new Kardia customers receive a free 30 day trial1 of Premium.

Please note: our KardiaBand product for Apple Watch REQUIRES Premium membership.
