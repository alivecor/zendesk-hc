How do I turn off Voice recordings for watch recordings?

You can turn off voice recording form watch readings by doing the following:

  1. Open the Kardia watch app
  2. "Force Touch" the app face by pressing down on screen firmly to bring up the menu
  3. Tap "Settings"
  4. Use the crown to scroll down to "Voice recordings"
  5.  Toggle off the setting
  6. Use the crown to scroll back up to the top of the screen
  7. Tap Close
