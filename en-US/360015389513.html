With regards to the above, please remember the return policy for resellers:

  • If returning the product within the 30 day return window, they must go directly to the store they purchased the device from.
  • If returning for a warranty exchange:
    • If they are still within the return window for the store they purchased from they should contact that store directly.
    • If they are outside the sellers return policy but within the 1 year warranty, the customer must provide a proof of purchase (such as a receipt) that reflects the date of purchase of the device is within the 1 year in order for us to accept the product back.
      • Amazon US and Amazon Canada are exceptions as the customer can provide us their Amazon order number and we can then escalate to Level 2 for them to confirm the order on their end prior to issuing an RMA.
      • Alivecor.com is of course also an exception as we can check the validity of the order in Shopify.
