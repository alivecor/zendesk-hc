How do I view, print or share my EKG as a PDF?

There are a few ways to access your EKG to view, print, or share it to your physician.

From the History List

  1. Tap the "History" button on the EKG Card on the home screen
  2. Scroll down to the ECG you would like to use.
  3. Tap the small envelope icon next to the reading.
  4. Select "Share PDF"
  5. Optionally choose to password protect your PDF. If not needed, simply select "Skip"
  6. Select the Export button in the top right hand corner. 
  7. Choose from your Device's options, how you would like to share the recording.

From the Kardia Instant Analysis result screen

  1. Complete an ECG reading
  2. Select "Share PDF"
  3. Optionally choose to password protect your PDF. If not needed, simply select "Skip"
  4. Select the Export button in the top right hand corner. 
  5. Choose from your Device's options, how you would like to share the recording.

Share Options

PDF

Choose this option to view your reading as a PDF on your mobile device. Subsequently you can share the PDF through any means available to your device when viewing it by tapping the share button in the top right of the screen when viewing the PDF.

Email

Choose this option to quickly create an email with the EKG PDF attached, and some information placed in the body of the email for you. This option is useful to help you populate your own records or to quickly send an EKG to your physician.

Print

This uses your mobile device's print ability to print directly to any device that is setup and compatible with your device. This is an Apple or Android system functionality.

Clinician Review

This option sends your EKG to one of our partner Clinicians for review. This is a paid service, and the options available to you are dependent on your location. The level of detail of each report type and turn around time are displayed here for your review. This option is useful if you feel that your Kardia Instant Analysis needs further review by a trained human professional. More information can be found here.
