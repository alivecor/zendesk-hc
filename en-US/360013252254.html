KardiaPro FAQ

What is KardiaPro?

  • KardiaPro is a service that allows doctors to be able to link directly with their patients and view their EKGs almost immediately after being taken.  Doctors will subscribe to the main administrative service, while patients will subscribe to the patient-level service.

How much does the KardiaPro service cost?

  • The cost is $15.00 per month for a patient to subscribe to KardiaPro.*
    • *Doctors can choose whether to have patients pay them directly, or pay Alivecor through the App.  Doctors who have their patients pay them directly have the option of choosing their price for the service.  They are not locked into the $15.00 default rate for customer's who pay AliveCor directly.

What is included with the KardiaPro service?

  • In addition to all features available with the Premium level service**, all EKGs taken by the patient will be automatically sent to their doctor for review immediately upon completion.
  • **Doctors can choose to disable certain features.  More on that later.

How does a customer sign-up for KardiaPro?

  • Once registered by their doctor, the customer will be sent a welcome email.  Contained within that email will be 12 character referral code.  The customer will need to sign-up for their Kardia account.  During the signup process it should ask them if they have a referral code.  They can also enter in the referral code by opening up the Account Management area (the shield icon at the upper-right hand corner of the Kardia app) and enter the referral code at the bottom of that window.

Are there any options that the doctor can override on the patient's Kardia App?

  • Yes, there are two things:
    • Doctors can choose how the patients pay for the service.  Doctors setup whether the patient pays AliveCor directly, or pays them directly.
      • *See prior comment above under cost of KardiaPro service
    • Doctors can also disable Clinician Reviews for any patients linked to them.
