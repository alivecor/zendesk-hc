How do I change the SmartRhythm notification frequency?

How often and how aggressively SmartRhythm Notifications are triggered can be adjusted from settings in the watch app. To make a change do the following:Â 

  1. Open the Kardia watch app
  2. "Force Touch" against the Kardia watch app screen by placing our finger on the screen and pressing against it with some force to bring up a menu
  3. Tap the "Settings" icon
  4. Scroll down to the bottom of the settings area to "SmartRhythm Sensitivity"
  5. Tap the plus or minus singe to adjust the green bar which designates a settings of low, medium or high sensitivity.
  6. Tap "Close"
