Why is my Height setting showing in centimeters on the Kardia App?

The Weight "Unit of Measure" setting in the Kardia App settings, also controls whether or not the Height setting will show in Imperial or Metric (Feet vs Centimeters).

Check the Weight Unit of Measure setting by:

  • Clicking on the App Menu (the three horizontal bars)
  • Clicking on Settings
  • Clicking on Weight Settings
  • Select either "Pounds" or "Kilograms" for the Unit of Measure.

If Pounds is selected, the height will show in Feet.Â  If Kilograms is selected, the height will show in Centimeters.

This option can also be changed in the App.alivecor.com website by clicking on your email address, selecting Profile, and then on the left side of the page selecting either "ft/lbs" or "cm/kg".
