What is the "GDPR" and how does it relate to AliveCor?

"GDPR" is an abbreviation for the General Data Protection Regulation in the European Union.  Below are some quick facts regarding the law.

Q: What is the General Data Protection Regulation?
A: In short, it makes one set of data protection rules for all companies operating in the EU, regardless of where they are based.

Q: When did the GDPR take effect?
A: The law was passed in April 2016 and became effective in May 2018.

Q: What affect does this new law have on AliveCor and our duties as CSRs?
A: International customers are now required to create a password for their EKGs whenever they generate a PDF of the EKG through the Kardia App.  Where customers in the US have the ability to skip this option, International customers can no longer do so.
