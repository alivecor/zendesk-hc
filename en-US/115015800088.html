How do I cancel Premium?

Premium can be canceled by you at any time from within the Kardia phone app.

To cancel do the following:

  1. Click on the icon at the top right corner of your Kardia app home screen
  2. Click the “Manage membership” button at the center of the right side area
  3. Tap “Cancel membership” and confirm “Yes” on the following screen.

Thats it, premium is now cancelled and you will no longer be charged a monthly or yearly fee.
