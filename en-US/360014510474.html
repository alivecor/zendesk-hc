Customer wants us to honor a discount/promo code that was/is not available when they went to purchase.

In cases where a customer wants us to honor a discount or promo code that was either:

  • Not yet in use but was started after they purchased their device(s), or
  • Expired prior to them purchasing their device

AliveCor will honor the discount/promo code for a period of 30 days prior to the start of the promo and 30 days after the end of the promo.

If a customer is asking us to honor a promo and they fall within the above stated guidelines, escalate the ticket to Level 2 for approval and confirmation that they will process the specified promo.
