Can Kardia Mobile or Kardia Band detect Arrhythmias other than AFIb?

Kardia has algorithms cleared by the FDA to tell you if your EKG is Normal or in Possible AFib. As a medical device, Kardia is not cleared to detect or communicate any other arrhythmias or rhythmsÂ 

That being said, the Kardia devices are single lead EKGs, therefore any Arrhythmia that is present in Lead I EKG can be present in your Kardia EKG recording. To have your EKG reviewed for other arrhythmias you may share them with your physician to review.
