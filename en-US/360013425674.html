Fields in the Insights report (Premium service only) such as "Diet" are not fields that are able to be input manually via the Kardia App.

Those types of fields are for customers that have their Kardia app linked up to their Apple Health/Google Fit accounts.Â  Having their accounts linked up with Kardia, allows the Kardia app to capture that type of information from Apple/Google and show them on the Insights report.
