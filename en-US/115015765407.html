MedPro Conversion to full Kardia Accounts

As of November 30th, 2017, the Kardia app automatically updated to include new features all available to you at no charge. Many of these features are part of our Premium membership—to which you have been granted free lifetime access thanks to your longtime association with our product.

One step to this upgrade, requires you to complete your Kardia account profile, the screen you are referring to. Once you complete this screen, you will have lifetime access to the following features:

  • Access to SmartRhythm monitoring, for those using the Kardia app with the Apple Watch®. Click here to learn more.
  • 30-day summary reports.
  • Blood pressure monitoring.
  • Tracking of resting heart rate, weight and medication.
  • Unlimited EKG cloud storage and history.
