How do I reset my password if I forgot it?

We all have to remember a lot of passwords. If you forgot your Kardia password, it’s easy to reset it. Just do the following:

  1. Open the Kardia app on your phone
  2. Tap Log In
  3. Tap Forgot password?
  4. Enter the email you used to create your Kardia account and submit
  5. Go to your email client and open the AliveCor password reset email within 60 minutes of the request and follow the instructions to choose a new password 
  6. Use the new password to Log In on your phone

If you don’t have access to that email address to complete the reset process, contact us at support@alivecor.com for assistance.
