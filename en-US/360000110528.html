Can I take an EKG if I have one hand?

 It is possible to record readings with only one hand. If you would like to record a Lead II ECG then:

  • Place the electrode that is closer to the top of your mobile device on your left knee
  • Place your right fingers on the electrode closer to the bottom of your mobile device.

 
In order to record an Anterior Precordial Lead ECG:

  • Place the Kardia Mobile on the lower side of the chest with the bottom of your mobile device toward the center of your body.
