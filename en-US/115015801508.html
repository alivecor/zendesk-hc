Can I download the Kardia app onto my Apple iPad?

The Kardia app is compatible with Apple iPads,  Here are the steps for downloading Kardia to your iPad:

  1. Open the App Store on your iPad
  2. Tap in the “Search” field and type “Kardia”
  3. Select the "Kardia" application, not "KardiaPro" which is a different application that your login will not work with.
