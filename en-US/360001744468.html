I am not getting Kardia Notifications on my Apple watch

If you think you may not be getting notifications on your watch from the Kardia app, the following steps may help.

Turn on notifications permissions for the App itself:

  1. Open the settings App
  2. Tap Notifications
  3. Scroll down and tap Kardia
  4. Toggle on “Allow Notifications” and the “Sounds” 
  5. Additionally you can turn on the rest of the notification types below

If you want daily reminders also check app settings:

  1. Open the Kardia App
  2. Make sure you're logged in
  3. Open the left side menu by tapping the three line icon at the top left corner of the screen
  4. Select "Settings"
  5. Go in to EKG, Medication, Weight, Blood Pressure areas and turn on notifications for any reminder you would like activated.

Set your watch to Mirror the phone settings

  1. Open the app named “watch” on your phone
  2. Scroll down and tap “Notifications”
  3. Scroll down to the “Kardia” app from the list of apps in the “Mirror iphone alerts from” list
  4. Make sure the toggle is green/on for the Kardia app

Set general alert settings for watch

  1. Open the app named “watch” on your phone
  2. Tap “Sounds and Haptics”
  3. Set the “Alert volume” to high by moving the slider to the far right
  4. Make sure “Silent Mode” is off
  5. Set “Haptic Strength” to high by moving the slider to the far right

Make sure watch is not in Do not Disturb or Theatre mode.

  1. Go to your watch
  2. Press in the crown to display the main watch face
  3. Slide up from the bottom to show the “Control Center” which is a series of buttons with different functions.
  4. Make sure the Moon symbol for Do Not DIsturb is not turned on (purple = on)
  5. Make sure the Bell symbol for SIlent mode is not turned on (red = on)
  6. Make sure the masks symbol for cinema mode is not turned on (orange = on)

If any of these settings were not set correctly, you may not have received the alerts.
