Why can I not see my first EKG?

For U.S. users, your first EKG will temporarily blocked from view as we process it and have it reviewed by a U.S Board-certified Cardiologist. We cover the cost of this review, you pay nothing.

Once the review is complete (within 24 hours, but usually less), you will have full access to EKG functionality.

This will only happen for your FIRST EKG. No future EKGs will have this delay.

After the first EKG:

  • You will not need to wait for a result, you will always get an immediate algorithm Kardia Instant Analysis that gives results including Possible AF and Normal
  • You will never have to pay for our Kardia Instant Analysis
  • You can always email your EKG top yourself or your physician
